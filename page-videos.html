Visa Mosaic

Spinifex Group, out of Los Angeles, reached out to me to create a video for them that would have updatable text and images.

I created this project in HTML5 using Hype 4. I worked with their developers to enable the text and images to be updatable with their CMS.

Nail Bar & Spa promo video

Nail Bar & Spa asked me to make a promotional video for their business in Maple Grove.

I was required to write the script, shoot the video, edit the video, and get the voice over recorded. I was able to do everything myself except the voice over, which I hired someone else to do for me.

I'm pretty happy with the final project as this is the first time I have had to act all of the roles in a production.

Postmodern Film

While attending college at the University of Minnesota Duluth another student and I applied for and received a grant to make a student film.

Under the supervision of Catherine Ishino we decided to make a film in the style of postmodern.

Kelen, my project partner, and I split responsibilities or writing, acting, filming, editing and everything else involved in creating a film.

It was a lot of fun and a lot of work. We learned a lot and would definitely do it all over again!
